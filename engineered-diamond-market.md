<p><ul><li>RVD Diamond Grain</li><li>MBD Diamond Grain</li><li>SCD Diamond Grain</li><li>SMD Diamond Grain</li><li>DMD Diamond Grain</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The engineered diamond market is categorized into several types based on grain characteristics. RVD (Regularly Sized Diamond) grains are commonly used for industrial applications due to their size consistency. MBD (Micro Diamond) grains are ultra-fine, ideal for precision cutting and polishing. SCD (Single Crystal Diamond) grains offer superior sharpness for high-performance tools. SMD (Synthetic Monocrystalline Diamond) grains provide enhanced durability for demanding applications. DMD (Diamond Micron Powder) grains are versatile, used in various abrasives and coatings for detailed work.</p></p>
