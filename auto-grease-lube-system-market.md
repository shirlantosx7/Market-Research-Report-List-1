<p><strong>The Auto Grease Lube System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct System</li><li>Indirect System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Auto Grease Lube System market comprises two main types: direct and indirect systems. The direct system delivers grease straight to the lubrication points, ensuring optimal application and minimal waste, making it ideal for high-precision machinery. In contrast, the indirect system uses a centralized unit to distribute grease through tubing to various points, offering flexibility and ease of maintenance. Together, these systems enhance equipment longevity and efficiency by ensuring consistent lubrication in automotive and industrial applications.</p></p>
