<p><ul><li>Analog Servo Driver</li><li>Digital Servo Driver</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The servo drive unit market is primarily divided into two types: analog servo drivers and digital servo drivers. Analog servo drivers utilize continuous signals to control motor movements, providing simpler and often cost-effective solutions for basic applications. In contrast, digital servo drivers process information in discrete steps, enhancing precision, flexibility, and control for complex tasks. Digital drives typically offer advanced features like programmable settings and feedback options, making them ideal for applications requiring high accuracy and real-time performance adjustments.</p></p>
