<p><strong>The Karl Fischer Oven Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Coulometric Titration</li><li>Volumetric Titration</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Karl Fischer Oven market includes two primary types of titration methods: coulometric and volumetric. Coulometric titration measures moisture content by generating iodine electrochemically, making it highly sensitive and suitable for low water concentrations. In contrast, volumetric titration involves adding a reagent (iodine solution) until the reaction reaches completion, suitable for higher moisture levels. Both methods are essential in various industries, including pharmaceuticals, food, and petrochemicals, for precise moisture analysis and quality control.</p></p>
