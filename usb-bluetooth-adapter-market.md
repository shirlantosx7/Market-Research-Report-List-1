<p><ul><li>Single-band Bluetooth Adapter</li><li>Dual-band Bluetooth Adapter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The USB Bluetooth adapter market comprises two primary types: single-band and dual-band adapters. Single-band Bluetooth adapters operate on the standard 2.4 GHz frequency, supporting basic connectivity and ideal for simple devices like keyboards and mice. In contrast, dual-band adapters offer enhanced performance by utilizing both 2.4 GHz and 5 GHz frequencies, leading to reduced interference and improved data transfer rates. This makes dual-band adapters suitable for high-demand applications such as audio streaming and gaming, catering to diverse user needs.</p></p>
