<p><p>The industrial ultrasonic transducer market consists of various types catering to distinct applications. Contact transducers make direct contact with surfaces for precise measurements. Dual element transducers employ two elements for enhanced sensitivity and accuracy. Immersion transducers are designed for submerged use, ideal for liquid testing. Shear wave transducers generate shear waves for testing materials with complex geometries. Delay line transducers utilize a delay medium to measure thickness or detect flaws, providing valuable insights in non-destructive testing environments.</p></p>
