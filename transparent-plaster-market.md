<p><p>The transparent plaster market is segmented into two main types: non-perforated and perforated transparent plaster. Non-perforated transparent plaster provides a seamless, smooth finish, ideal for applications where aesthetic appeal and structural integrity are paramount. It offers enhanced durability and moisture resistance. In contrast, perforated transparent plaster features small openings that promote air circulation and moisture management, making it advantageous in environments prone to humidity. Both types cater to diverse architectural needs and preferences.</p></p>
