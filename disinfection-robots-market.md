<p><p>The disinfection robots market primarily comprises UV-C and HPV technologies. UV-C robots utilize ultraviolet light to eliminate pathogens on surfaces, effectively targeting bacteria and viruses without chemicals. In contrast, HPV (Hydrogen Peroxide Vapor) robots deploy vaporized hydrogen peroxide to disinfect larger areas, ensuring thorough penetration into hard-to-reach spaces. Both technologies are vital in healthcare settings and beyond, promoting hygiene and safety, particularly in environments requiring rigorous infection control measures. Each type offers unique benefits suited to various disinfection needs.</p></p>
