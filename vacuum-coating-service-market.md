<p><ul><li>PVD Coating Service</li><li>CVD Coating Service</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vacuum coating service market primarily includes two key types: Physical Vapor Deposition (PVD) and Chemical Vapor Deposition (CVD) services. PVD coating involves the physical transition of materials from a solid to a vapor state, which then condense on surfaces to create thin films, ideal for enhancing durability and aesthetics. Conversely, CVD coating involves chemical reactions in a vapor phase to produce coatings with exceptional properties. Both methods are widely used across industries like electronics, automotive, and optics for performance enhancement.</p></p>
