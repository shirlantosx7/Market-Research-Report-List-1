<p><p>The medical ultrasound devices market is divided into two primary types: diagnostic ultrasound systems and therapeutic ultrasound systems. Diagnostic ultrasound systems are utilized for imaging and examining various conditions within the body, employing sound waves to create detailed visual representations of organs and tissues. In contrast, therapeutic ultrasound systems focus on treatment applications such as physiotherapy and surgery, leveraging ultrasound waves to promote tissue healing, alleviate pain, and enhance drug delivery. Together, these systems play a crucial role in modern healthcare.</p></p>
