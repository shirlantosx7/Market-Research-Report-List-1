<p><strong>The Diving Bell Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wet Bell</li><li>Closed Bell</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Diving bell market types include wet bells and closed bells, each serving distinct operational needs in underwater exploration. Wet bells are open systems that allow water to enter, facilitating air exchange and quick immersion, often used for shorter dives. In contrast, closed bells are sealed units, maintaining an internal air supply and pressure, which enables longer and deeper dives while providing more safety against decompression sickness. Both types cater to commercial, research, and construction diving applications, ensuring efficiency and safety.</p></p>
