<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The QLED Display Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>QLED</li><li>QDEF</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The QLED display market primarily comprises two types: traditional QLED and Quantum Dot Enhancement Film (QDEF) displays. QLED utilizes quantum dots infused in an LED backlight to enhance color and brightness, offering superior picture quality and vivid colors. In contrast, QDEF employs a layer of quantum dots placed over an LCD panel to improve color accuracy and intensity without being an independent display technology. Both markets cater to different consumer needs, focusing on premium viewing experiences and innovative display technologies.</p></p>
