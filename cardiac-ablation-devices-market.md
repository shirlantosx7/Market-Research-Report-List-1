<p><p>The cardiac ablation devices market includes two primary types: multielectrode ablation devices and single point ablation devices. Multielectrode ablation devices utilize multiple electrodes to simultaneously target various ablation sites, enhancing precision and efficiency in treating arrhythmias. In contrast, single point ablation devices focus on one specific area, allowing for targeted therapy but potentially requiring more time for comprehensive treatment. Both device types contribute to the growing landscape of minimally invasive procedures for cardiac arrhythmia management.</p></p>
