<p><p>The Fingerprint Sensor on Display market features two primary technologies: optical and ultrasonic sensors. Optical sensors capture fingerprint images using light, allowing for higher resolution and vibrant display integration but can be less effective under certain conditions, like moisture. In contrast, ultrasonic sensors utilize sound waves to capture detailed 3D fingerprint data, enhancing security and performance, particularly in varied environmental conditions. Both types are increasingly integrated into smartphones, improving user authentication while maximizing display real estate.</p></p>
