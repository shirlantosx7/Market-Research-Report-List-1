<p><strong>The Microfluidic PCR Chip Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Microchamber PCR</li><li>Continuous Flow PCR</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The microfluidic PCR chip market is categorized into two main types: microchamber PCR and continuous flow PCR. Microchamber PCR utilizes discrete reaction chambers to achieve amplification, providing precise control over reaction conditions and enabling simultaneous analysis of multiple samples. In contrast, continuous flow PCR involves a continuous loop system where the sample flows through a series of thermal zones, allowing for rapid cycling and enhanced throughput. Both methods aim to optimize PCR efficiency, reduce reagent usage, and improve diagnostic accuracy.</p></p>
