<p><p>The video intercom kit market is primarily divided into two types: Analog and IP. Analog types rely on traditional circuitry and are simpler, often used in small residences or buildings where basic communication is sufficient. In contrast, IP types utilize internet protocol technology, enabling higher quality video and audio, remote access, and integration with smart home systems. IP systems cater to larger, more complex installations, offering enhanced features like mobile connectivity and advanced security options, making them suitable for modern, tech-savvy users.</p></p>
