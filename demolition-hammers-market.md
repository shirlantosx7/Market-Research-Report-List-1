<p><p>Demolition hammers are categorized into three main types: electric, hydraulic, and pneumatic. Electric demolition hammers are powered by electricity, offering ease of use and portability for lighter tasks. Hydraulic hammers utilize fluid power, delivering high force and efficiency in heavy-duty applications. Pneumatic hammers operate using compressed air, providing strong power and portability, often favored in construction sites. Each type caters to specific demolition needs, balancing power, efficiency, and operational convenience across various applications.</p></p>
