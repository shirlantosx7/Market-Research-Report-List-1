<p><strong>The Semiconductor Test Handler Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gravity Handlers</li><li>Turret Handlers</li><li>Pick-and-Place Handlers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor test handler market comprises three main types: gravity handlers, turret handlers, and pick-and-place handlers. Gravity handlers utilize gravitational force to move and position devices, providing efficient handling for bulk applications. Turret handlers feature a rotating turret mechanism, allowing for high-speed processes and increased throughput by optimizing test cycles. Pick-and-place handlers use robotic arms to precisely pick semiconductors and place them in test sockets, offering flexibility and accuracy in various testing environments. Together, these handlers enhance the efficiency and effectiveness of semiconductor testing.</p></p>
