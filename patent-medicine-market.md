<p><p>The patent medicine market can be divided into two main types: product patents and process patents. Product patents protect the specific formulation or composition of a medicinal product, granting exclusive rights to the inventor for a set period. Process patents, on the other hand, cover the methods or processes used to create a medicinal product, allowing inventors to secure a competitive advantage through unique manufacturing techniques. Both types encourage innovation, ensuring that new medicines can be developed and marketed effectively.</p></p>
