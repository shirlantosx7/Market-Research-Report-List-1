Woven fiber silt curtains are made from interlaced threads, providing enhanced durability and effectiveness in retaining sediments. In contrast, non-woven fiber curtains use a matting process that allows for better filtration and easier installation. Both types are crucial in minimizing sediment dispersion during construction, dredging, and remediation activities, ensuring compliance with environmental regulations and protecting aquatic ecosystems.</p></p>
