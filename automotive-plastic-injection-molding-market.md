<p><p>The automotive plastic injection molding market encompasses various types aimed at producing lightweight, durable parts. Thermoplastic injection molding involves using thermoplastics that can be repeatedly melted and reshaped, making it versatile. Cold runner molding utilizes a system where the plastic cools and solidifies in the runner, minimizing waste. In contrast, hot runner molding keeps the plastic in a molten state, improving efficiency. Other methods may include specialized techniques catered to specific applications, enhancing design flexibility and production efficiency in the automotive sector.</p></p>
