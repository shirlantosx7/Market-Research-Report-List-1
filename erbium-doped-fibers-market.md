<p><p>The Erbium-Doped Fiber market is primarily categorized into two types: Single-Mode (SM) fiber and Polarization-Maintaining (PM) fiber. SM fibers are designed for long-distance communication, offering high bandwidth and low signal loss, making them ideal for telecommunications. In contrast, PM fibers are engineered to maintain the polarization of light, crucial for applications in sensitive measurements and high-performance systems, such as fiber optic sensors and quantum communications. Both types cater to distinct needs within the growing optical fiber market.</p></p>
