<p><p>Platelet antibody screening for transfusion involves methods to detect antibodies against platelet antigens, which can significantly impact transfusion outcomes. The market comprises two main types: antibody screening, which identifies antibodies that may cause transfusion reactions, ensuring safer transfusions; and crossmatching, which assesses compatibility between donor platelets and recipient blood. Both processes are critical in preventing alloimmunization and ensuring the efficacy of platelet transfusions, fostering improved patient safety and outcomes in transfusion medicine.</p></p>
