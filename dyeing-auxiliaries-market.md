<p><p>The dyeing auxiliaries market is segmented into various types, including dispersing agents, sequestering agents, scouring agents, wetting agents, and others. Dispersing agents help evenly distribute dyes in the solution, ensuring consistent color application. Sequestering agents bind metal ions, preventing undesirable chemical reactions. Scouring agents remove impurities from textiles, preparing them for dyeing. Wetting agents enhance the penetration of dyes into fibers, promoting uniformity. Other agents may include leveling agents and pH regulators, each serving specialized roles in the dyeing process.</p></p>
