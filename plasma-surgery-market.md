<p><p>The plasma surgery market comprises three key types: Neutral Plasma Surgery Systems, Plasma Ablation Systems, and Cold Plasma Surgical Systems. Neutral Plasma Surgery Systems utilize ionized gas to achieve precise tissue modification with minimal thermal damage. Plasma Ablation Systems focus on the removal of tissue by vaporization via plasma energy, suitable for various surgical applications. Cold Plasma Surgical Systems employ low-temperature plasma to achieve hemostasis and promote wound healing, enhancing surgical outcomes while reducing complications and recovery time.</p></p>
