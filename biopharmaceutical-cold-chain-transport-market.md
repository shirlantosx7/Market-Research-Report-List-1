<p><p>The biopharmaceutical cold chain transport market consists of three primary types: ground shipping, sea shipping, and air shipping. Ground shipping involves the use of trucks for short to medium distances, ensuring temperature control throughout the journey. Sea shipping is ideal for long-distance transport, utilizing refrigerated containers for bulk shipments, though it may take longer. Air shipping offers the fastest option for urgent deliveries, using temperature-controlled aircraft to maintain the integrity of sensitive biopharmaceutical products during transit.</p></p>
