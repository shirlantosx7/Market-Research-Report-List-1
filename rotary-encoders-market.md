<p><strong>The Rotary Encoders Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Incremental Rotary Encoders</li><li>Absolute Rotary Encoders</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rotary encoders market is primarily divided into two types: incremental and absolute rotary encoders. Incremental rotary encoders measure position changes relative to a starting point, providing feedback on movement direction and distance but requiring a reference reset. In contrast, absolute rotary encoders deliver a unique position value for each shaft angle, maintaining precise positioning even after power loss. These encoder types are crucial in various applications, including industrial automation, robotics, and consumer electronics, due to their reliability and accuracy.</p></p>
