<p><p>The aerospace winglets market comprises various designs tailored for aerodynamic efficiency. Blended winglets feature a smooth curve transitioning from the wing, enhancing lift and reducing drag. Elliptical winglets minimize vortex drag with their rounded shape. Raked winglets extend backward, improving performance at higher speeds. Sharklet winglets combine aesthetics and efficiency for fuel savings. Split scimitar winglets have an additional downward element, maximizing lift. Wingtip fences are vertical extensions that inhibit vortex formation. Other designs may include innovative hybrid solutions.</p></p>
