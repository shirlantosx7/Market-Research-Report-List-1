<p><strong>The Dye Sub Card Printers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ReTransfer</li><li>Direct to Card</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dye sub card printers are categorized into two main types: retransfer and direct-to-card. Retransfer printers print an image onto a film, which is then fused onto the card, ensuring high-resolution output and durability. This method is ideal for cards requiring vibrant colors and intricate designs. In contrast, direct-to-card printers print images directly onto the card surface, offering faster results and lower costs, making them suitable for high-volume applications where speed is essential and image quality is less critical.</p></p>
