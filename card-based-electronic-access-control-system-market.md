<p><strong>The Card-Based Electronic Access Control System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Standalone Access Control Systems</li><li>Networked Access Control Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Card-based electronic access control systems consist of two primary market types: standalone access control systems and networked access control systems. Standalone systems operate independently, managing access through a single door or location without the need for a centralized network, making them suitable for smaller applications. In contrast, networked access control systems connect multiple doors and entry points through a centralized server, allowing for centralized management, real-time monitoring, and scalability, making them ideal for larger facilities or organizations with complex access needs.</p></p>
