<p><p>The industrial magnetrons market is primarily segmented into two types: Continuous Wave (CW) and Pulsed magnetrons. Continuous Wave magnetrons provide a steady output of microwave power, suitable for applications such as heating, radar, and plasma generation. They offer stable performance for prolonged operations. In contrast, Pulsed magnetrons emit bursts of microwave energy, which are advantageous for applications requiring high peak power, like radar and material processing.
