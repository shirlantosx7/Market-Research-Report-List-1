<p><p>Aviation fueling systems are categorized into fixed installation and portable markets based on their application and mobility. Fixed installation systems are permanently installed at airports or fueling stations, designed for high-capacity and efficient fueling operations. These systems often include underground storage tanks and extensive piping networks. In contrast, portable aviation fueling systems are designed for flexibility and mobility, allowing for ground handling at remote locations or during emergencies. They typically consist of mobile tanks and fueling units that can be easily transported.</p></p>
