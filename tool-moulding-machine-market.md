<p><p>The tool moulding machine market is categorized into three main types: hydraulic, mechanical, and electric. Hydraulic moulding machines use fluid pressure to operate, offering high force and flexibility for complex shapes. Mechanical machines rely on mechanical components, providing simplicity and reliability for consistent production. Electric moulding machines utilize servo motors for precise control and energy efficiency, making them ideal for high-precision applications. Each type caters to different manufacturing needs, balancing factors like cost, speed, and operational complexity.</p></p>
