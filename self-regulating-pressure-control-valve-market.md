<p><ul><li>Pressure Regulators</li><li>Pressure Relief</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The self-regulating pressure control valve market encompasses two primary types: pressure regulators and pressure relief valves. Pressure regulators maintain a consistent output pressure by automatically adjusting flow in response to varying inlet pressures, ensuring stable performance in various applications. Pressure relief valves, on the other hand, protect systems from overpressure by releasing excess fluid or gas, preventing potential damage. Together, these devices ensure safety and efficiency in fluid control systems across industries like oil and gas, manufacturing, and HVAC.</p></p>
