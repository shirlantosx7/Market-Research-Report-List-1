<p><p>The anti-bark collar market includes various types of bark control devices designed to reduce excessive barking in dogs. Spray bark control uses a citronella or unscented spray to deter barking by creating an unpleasant mist. Ultrasonic bark control emits high-frequency sounds that are audible to dogs but not humans, discouraging barking. Static shock bark control delivers a mild electric shock as a correction. Other types may include vibration collars or collar sensors, offering alternative methods for behavior modification.</p></p>
