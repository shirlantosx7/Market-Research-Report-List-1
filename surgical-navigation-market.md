<p><p>The surgical navigation market comprises various technologies that enhance the precision of surgical procedures. Optical surgical navigation systems utilize cameras and visual markers to track surgical instruments in real-time, offering high accuracy in procedures like neurosurgery and orthopedic surgery. Electromagnetic surgical navigation systems rely on magnetic fields and sensors to determine the position of instruments, providing flexibility in surgeries where line-of-sight may be limited. Both systems aim to improve patient safety and outcomes through advanced positioning guidance.</p></p>
