<p><p>The waterproof bitumen membrane market is primarily divided into two types: SBS (Styrene-Butadiene-Styrene) and APP (Atactic Polypropylene). SBS membranes exhibit excellent elasticity and flexibility, making them ideal for areas with temperature fluctuations. Conversely, APP membranes are known for their robustness and resistance to UV radiation and extreme temperatures, providing long-lasting protection. Both types are widely used in construction and roofing applications to ensure effective waterproofing, durability, and protection against environmental elements.</p></p>
