<p><p>Magnetic fluid seals are innovative sealing solutions used in semiconductor manufacturing to prevent contamination and maintain vacuum conditions. Hollow shaft seals are designed for rotating shafts, utilizing magnetic fluids to create a dynamic seal that can handle variations in speed and pressure. Solid shaft seals, on the other hand, are used for non-rotating applications, providing a reliable barrier against particles and fluids. Both types ensure high performance, reduced wear, and increased equipment longevity in sensitive semiconductor environments.</p></p>
