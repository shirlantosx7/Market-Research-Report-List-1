<p><p>The healthcare wastewater treatment market consists of several primary types: </p><p>1. **Primary Treatment** involves the physical separation of solids from liquid waste, typically through sedimentation. </p><p>2. **Secondary Biological Purification** utilizes biological processes to degrade organic matter and pathogens, improving water quality. </p><p>3. **Tertiary Treatment** further polishes the water, employing advanced methods such as filtration and disinfection to remove residual contaminants. </p><p>4. **Others** may include specialized treatments like membrane filtration or advanced oxidation, catering to specific waste characteristics or regulatory requirements.</p></p>
