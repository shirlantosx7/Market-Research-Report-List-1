<p><ul><li>"Line Scan Camera"</li><li>"Area Scan Camera"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The RGB color camera market is segmented into two main types: line scan cameras and area scan cameras. Line scan cameras capture images one line at a time, making them ideal for applications requiring high resolution and continuous movement, such as quality control in manufacturing. In contrast, area scan cameras capture a full image at once, making them suitable for static scenes or tasks needing rapid processing, like facial recognition or surveillance. Both types serve unique applications in various industries.</p></p>
